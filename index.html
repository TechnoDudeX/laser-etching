How It Works

Three steps from your idea to a finished product on your desk.

1

Tell us what you need

You send us your logo, quantities, and any design preferences. We'll put together a custom mockup within 48 hours.

2

Approve your mockup

We send a digital proof for your review. One round of revisions included. Once you approve, production starts.

3

We produce and ship

Your order is laser-etched, hand-finished, and shipped directly to you. Local GTA pickup available.
